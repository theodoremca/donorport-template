      Criteria

      Donorport follows the three step process outline below.

       Every Organization that posts to Donorport has submitted an application.
      • Donorport reviews all requests for Organization accounts in order to maintain its reputation of transparency and trust. If you would like to post items for donors to sponsor, you must first submit an application for review and approval.

      • Step 1

        Your Organization completes and submits the application form here.

      • Step 2

        Donorport reviews the application and confirms the accuracy of the submission.
        • Is the Organization officially registered and in good standing?
        • Does the Organization adhere to the Terms of Service of Donorport?
        • Is the application contact a representative for the Organization?

      • Step 3

        An Administrator account is set up for the approved Organization where they may begin to post items.
